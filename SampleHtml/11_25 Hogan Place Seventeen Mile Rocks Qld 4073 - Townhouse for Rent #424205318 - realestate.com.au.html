11/25 Hogan Place Seventeen Mile Rocks

Secure and Private

In a leafy peaceful, complex totally secure from the outside you'll be pleased to call this place home.

Well designed across two levels you'll appreciate that this townhouse boasts 3 bedrooms and 2.5 bathrooms, allowing guests to have separate bathroom use.

When you enter the property you'll find an open plan kitchen, lounge and dining, flowing through to a very spacious courtyard.

The courtyard has a large tree which offers shade and privacy. Within the complex you'll find a resort style BBQ area beside the pool.

The property is air-conditioned and has ceiling fans and built in wardrobes throughout; with the main enjoying an ensuite. Come along and inspect.

Features:
* Air-conditioning
* Leafy patio
* Secure complex
* Pool on site
* Secure garage

Location:
